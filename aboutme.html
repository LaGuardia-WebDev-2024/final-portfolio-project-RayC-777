About Me

Hi! My names Reina. I am a Bronx NY native, but my family and I are from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I have lived in the South Bronx since I was born, and still do with my mother and two cats, Ali and Jasmine. Since a young age, I always showed a love for singing and dancing. I was singing at my church for many years in worship, something I have a deep connection to and still do. I was always exposed to the arts (and a plethora of disney movies), where the beginnings of my passion for musical theatre started. She startd bringing me to shows when I was around age six because of my focus on the performances. I loved seeing the Rockettes Christmas Spectacular, and thats how my dance path started. I begged my mom to put me in classes, and she helped me start tap, ballet, and jazz. When I got into elementarty school, I did my first musical in the fourth grade, and realized that it was the sign I needed to pursue musical theatre. I started taking voice lessons, and some acting-preparing me for middle school where I would study as a dance major for three years.

I began learnign other styles such as hip-hop, West African, and even some ballroom outside of school. After graduating, I was accepted into Fiorello LaGuardia High as a Drama major, where I polished my craft in dance, drama, and singing. I have had the privelige (to pee) having the oppourutnity to be trusted with roles like Pennywise in Urinetown: The Musical and Estella, a Shark girl in our Mainstage of 2025 West Side Story. I have been blessed with incredible teachers who did nothing but push me to the extent, effort, and passionfor this performing arts career I hope to give and pursue in the future. I have also been given the oppourutnity to continue studying MT in a B.F.A program with Marymount Manhattan College! I have no doubt ij my mind that I willbe inspired by the artisyty the school ainms to acheve, and am very excited to start the next part of my journey there. Before then, though, I am content with spending days at home with my mother, reaading, and sipping mate . Most of all, I am grateful for the support system I have in my family and friends, who have been with me through every step of this journey, and can't wait to see whats next, Lord's willing.
